Brentwood Dance School



Welcome to the Brentwood Dance School.
We are a non-profit dance program benefiting the Brentwood School District's elementary schools PTO since 1980. Classes each year alternate between McGrath Elementary School and Mark Twain Elementary, in the gymnasium. PreK thru 6th grade classes include ballet and tap instruction. 7th -12th grade include tap and jazz with ballet technique as an option.
